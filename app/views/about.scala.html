What is it?

HWHelp was built to help students to solve math and science problems. However the only tool currently offered is the matrix calculator & temperature converter, but more may come in the future...

Who built it?

My name is Akash Melethil; I am the founder of SkyArc Studios. I built this during my spare time, while I am not at university or building other stuff.

How was it built?

The front-end is built using HTML,CSS and Javascript, the backend is built using java.
